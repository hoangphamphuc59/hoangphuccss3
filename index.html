Final Project - Color Gallery

Welcome to my color gallery. This site showcases a variety of beautiful and vibrant colors, each with its own unique personality and emotion. You'll find a spectrum ranging from the deepest reds to the brightest whites.

The layout of this gallery is fully responsive, utilizing CSS Grid to provide an optimal viewing experience on any device. Whether you are browsing on a mobile phone, tablet, or a large desktop monitor, the images adapt seamlessly to your screen. Furthermore, the design respects your system preferences for motion and color scheme, offering a customized and accessible experience.
